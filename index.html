learn konkani

basic words

  • hau or hav- the person reading this or a thing
  • muje- of hau/haav
  • tu-who you are talking to
  • tuje-of tu
  • asai-being
  • kaso-what/how kaso asai is how are you
  • to-he
  • tei-she
  • tee-they
  • boro-good
  • paad-bad
  • asa-have or be(adjective) haav boro asa is "i am good or it is good "

sentences

some words have more than one meaning in a sentence
